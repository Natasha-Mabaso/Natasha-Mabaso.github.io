HILLVALLEY DAYCARE

At Hillvalley Daycare we pride ourselves in providing excellent care and out standing level of basic foundation for your child’s education. Our medium of instruction is English.

Offering:

  • A hands-on learning experience – Children learn most effectively when directly engaged visually, practically and in a fun way.
  • Special attention – Our classes are limited only to a certain number children so we can provide child specific learning for special needs.
  • Good nutrition – We provide well balanced 3 meals a day to ensure that every child gets a nutritious diet.
  • Play and Fun – We ensure that children alternate learning with play & interactive games to stimulate their minds and also learn to get along with other children.
  • Health and Safety – We have a facility to care for children in cases of minor accidents that may happen during play.

Our operating hours are as follows:

Operating Hours: 6am - 6pm.
After-care hours: 6pm - 7pm.

Would you like to register your child?

All parents are required to pay the annual registration fee of R350.00 which will be used to purchase stationery, aqueous cream, toilet rolls, hand wash and wipes that will be used by all children, for the whole year.

The fees are as follows:
Children 0 - 2 of age you pay R1000.00 which is required every month before the 1st day.
Children 3 - 5 of age you pay R800.00 which is required every month before the 1st day. If two children are from the same home, you get R200 discount.

We also provide aftercare for school going children whose parents knock off late, at the amount of R200.00 per month, which includes sandwich and juice.

Our day-care provides transport starting from R150.00 per child depending on the distance, which is paid cash on the 1st of each month.
